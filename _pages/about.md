Hi there! I'm Helin Cao (in Chinese: 曹贺淋).

I am currently a Robot Perception Expert at [Neura Robotics](https://neura-robotics.com/), where I actively contribute to foundational ecosystems like [NeuraGym](https://neura-robotics.com/neuraverse/) and [NeuraVerse](https://neuraverse.com/), bridging the gap between large-scale spatial simulation and physical robotic intelligence.

I am also concluding my Ph.D. at the [Center for Robotics, University of Bonn](https://www.robotics.uni-bonn.de/) and the [Lamarr Institute](https://lamarr-institute.org/), advised by [Prof. Sven Behnke](https://www.ais.uni-bonn.de/behnke/) in the [Autonomous Intelligent Systems group](https://www.ais.uni-bonn.de/). I was a proud core member of Team [NimbRo@Home](https://www.ais.uni-bonn.de/nimbro/@Home/), deploying perception systems in international competitions. Prior to this, I spent three wonderful years earning my Master's at [TU Munich](https://www.tum.de), where I had the privilege of working with [Prof. Matthias Niessner](https://niessnerlab.org/members/matthias_niessner/profile.html) and [Prof. Eckehard Steinbach](https://www.professoren.tum.de/en/steinbach-eckehard) at the [Visual Computing Group](https://niessnerlab.org/index.html).

My research vision is to endow machines with a native, physics-grounded understanding of the 3D world. Currently, my work and interests center around:

- **Physics-grounded world models:** Moving beyond visual pixel prediction to simulate underlying physical dynamics (e.g., gravity, collisions) in latent spaces, creating a "commonsense brain" for embodied agents.
- **Interactive 4D scene representations:** Advancing 3D Gaussian Splatting (3DGS) and Neural Fields into dynamic, interactable environments enriched with semantic occupancy for precise robotic manipulation.
- **Spatial computing & sim-to-real infrastructure:** Architecting high-fidelity digital twin engines and scalable spatial data pipelines to bridge the critical "data gap" in Embodied AI.
